Strategic Design Philosophy

The new website should embody the same qualities that define Emily's photography.

Natural.
Elegant.
Authentic.
Timeless.

The design language should avoid overly trendy aesthetics, excessive animation and visual clutter. Instead, it should focus on clean layouts, strong imagery, emotional storytelling and intuitive navigation.

The overall experience should feel premium without feeling inaccessible.

When visitors land on the website, they should immediately understand three things:

Who Emily helps.

What makes her different.

How to take the next step.

Homepage Redesign Strategy

The homepage will become the primary conversion page within the website.

Its role is no longer to display photography. Its role is to create trust and guide users toward enquiry.

Hero Section

The first screen should immediately communicate positioning and value.

Large full-width lifestyle image.

Authentic family, pet or lifestyle photography.

No sliders.

No multiple rotating images.

One powerful image.

Proposed Headline:

Natural Light & Authentic Lifestyle Photography Across the UAE

Supporting Headline:

Timeless photography for families, pets, entrepreneurs and meaningful life moments. Captured naturally. Remembered forever.

Primary CTA:

Book a Consultation

Secondary CTA:

View Portfolio

Positioning Section

Immediately below the hero section, visitors should encounter a concise explanation of what makes EHRAY Photography different.

Most photographers compete through editing styles.

EHRAY Photography should compete through authenticity.

This section should communicate Emily's philosophy around natural light, genuine moments and timeless imagery.

The objective is to create emotional differentiation rather than technical differentiation.

Featured Portfolio Section

Rather than displaying dozens of images, the homepage should showcase a carefully curated collection of standout work.

The focus should be quality over quantity.

The portfolio should be organized into four primary categories:

Families

Pets

Personal Branding

This section should include:

Featured client experiences

The objective is to reduce uncertainty and build confidence.

Service Category Section

Visitors should immediately understand what services are offered.

The homepage should present four primary services as the core brand offering:

Family Photography

Pet Photography

Personal Branding Photography

About Emily Section

This section should focus on human connection.

The objective is not to present a professional biography.

Lead Capture System

The redesigned website should include multiple enquiry opportunities throughout the customer journey.

WhatsApp should remain visible throughout the website using a floating action button.

Many UAE customers prefer immediate messaging over traditional forms.

Contact Forms

Every service page should include a dedicated enquiry form.

The forms should collect:

Name

Phone Number

Email

Session Type

Preferred Date

Additional Information

This reduces friction and increases enquiries.

This is particularly valuable for personal branding and event clients.

Technical SEO Framework

The redesigned website will implement a complete technical SEO foundation.

This includes optimized page titles, meta descriptions, structured heading hierarchy, image optimization and internal linking architecture.

Schema markup will be implemented to help search engines understand the business, services, reviews and location information.

Website speed optimization will ensure fast loading times across desktop and mobile devices.

Mobile optimization will receive particular attention given that the majority of traffic is expected to originate from smartphones.

Image compression, lazy loading, modern code structure and performance optimization techniques will be implemented to improve both user experience and search engine rankings.

Expected Outcomes

Upon completion of Phase 2, EHRAY Photography will possess a modern, conversion-focused website that functions as a business development asset rather than a portfolio archive.

Visitors will clearly understand the brand's positioning, trust the photographer more quickly and encounter multiple opportunities to enquire.

The website will provide the digital foundation required for future social media campaigns, paid advertising initiatives, referral programs and organic search growth.

The objective is not to create a more attractive website.

The objective is to create a website that consistently generates enquiries, builds trust and supports the long-term growth of EHRAY Photography.
